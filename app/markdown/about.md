# Why would you possibly blog?

Hank Moody had a hard time understanding the blogging kind, me too. Thinking that my own brain juice might be of interest for anybody else always felt almost arrogant and utterly wrong.

So what's this after all? Well, I like playing around. Playing with technology, coding along and building stuff, abusing good stuff. For one this is my private playground. Furthermore, sharing is fun. Putting out some music, books and thoughts can't cause no harm.
Lastly you can always blame me for just playing due to vanity and for reasons of self-marketing.

Trust me, I am just writing up on things so I can memorize them better.
