# Discarded Science

## Ideas That Seemed Good at the Time

Oh science. You're always portrayed as being so linear, so consecutively constructing truth. I feel like you've been lying to me and examples do not end with a flat earth or heliocentrism.
13 ideas which seemed good at a time but turned out to be awkfully off and immensely entertaining by now.
Ideas start from the flat and hollow earth and continue with cannibalism, telegony, cold fusion and end at ideas about Venusians. Just considering the fact that the Nazis seemd to believe partly in a hollow earth and were convinced that if they shot a rocket high enough it would break the sky, makes this book a good bathroom read.
