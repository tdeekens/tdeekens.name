# Farewell, My Lovely

This guy can write, Chandler's lines are highly addictive. Take this on your holiday, take a sunbath and wake up with a sunburn. You might have forgotten anything around you for a few hours.
It's 1941 and a privat dectective by the name of Marlowe is hired by a bankrobber to find a necklace. Luckily the bankrobber was just released from prision and finding a nicklace can mean digging for some dirt. Nobody wants to talk and at some point Marlow himself is suspected of being the actual necklace's thief and killer of an informant. I should not spoil more.
